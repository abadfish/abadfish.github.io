About Me

From a small horse town in upstate NY, an every growing curiosity for the world took me to places I never knew existed, but it's the world of machines that fascinates me the most.

Full-stack-educated at The Flatiron School in New York, NY, and formal Bachelor of Business Administration from Hoftra University in Hempstead, NY. Before finding software development as a passion, I had a ten year professional career in trading for a large broker-dealer and a few funds. I've been at an IT company called Canaan Technology in Norwalk, CT for a little over a year while a student at The Flatiron School. I've written a couple of my own trading algorithms as well as done some freelance web design.
