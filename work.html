Where I've been has made me who I am today.

  • Canaan Technology

    Canaan is my first job in the tech industry. Here, I design and implement custom data management solutions,
    program and deploy full spectrum network solutions for residential, retail and corporate spaces and
    manage fully outsourced IT solutions for data servers, workstations, IP and digital phone systems, exchange servers.
    Additionally, I handle development and customization of communication compliance solutions for financial client base.

  • World Macro

    World Macro is a venture I started with a former colleague from the trading world, Darren Brogden.
    The site is a subscription-based service offering access to Darren's Portfolio, his technical trading patterns and expressions, and current news headlines.
    This is my first full site, written in Ruby on Rails and jQuery, fully deployed on Heroku, and generating income.

  • Greenwich Harbour Capital

    GHC is a hedge fund based in Greenwich, CT, trading an interest rate swap arbitrage program.
    Here I discovered my passion for coding when I was exposed to Visual Basic for Applications.
    Other responsibilities there were model maintenance, algorithmic market monitor development and trade processing.
    I also facilitated upgrades of both master and exchange servers for the fund.

  • Thoroughbred Futures Fund

    For the flagship fund of Managed Capital Advisory Group I did all research and due diligence and built and maintained the portfolio of funds.
    I also built custom portfolios for clients upon broker request.
    Aside from risk management and strategy, I also published weekly commentary and maintained an active blog site for our investors.

  • Marex Spectron

    Marex is a British brokerage firm specializing in futures.
    Here I was responsible for fundamental macroeconomic & technical market analysis and translation to rate product trading.
    I published daily and weekly group commentary discussing current analysis, flows, and trade ideas.
    I also developed and executed rate futures option strategies and hedge trades for cash fixed income market makers.

  • Citi Global Markets

    My longest tenure, I was a trading analyst in Futures Execution.
    Here I executed American, European, and Asian interest rate option strategies.
    I specialized in LIBOR derivative products as well as products further out the curve and swaps.

  • New York State Legislature

    I had two separate tenures with the NYS Legislature, one with the Assembly and then another with the Senate.
    I worked as a legislative aid as well as playing a role in constituent services and community events.
    Admittedly these were my least favorite work experiences, however, they gave me a very solid background in people skills and discipline that I am most grateful for.
